Emailing Entries & Media

It's just too easy. Select an entry and press the Email toolbar button. Journler launches your preferred email client and automatically sets both the email's subject to the entry title and the email's body to the entry's plain text content.

When a single entry is selected Journler includes only the text that is selected within that entry. If the entry includes media and your email client is Apple Mail Journler also attaches the media to the email. You can also email media by itself. Select a file or other media in the Resource Pane and press the same Email toolbar button.

Not only can you mail your entries and documents from Journler, you can also copy existing emails into your entries and view them from within the program. Read more.

iWeb

Journler works with iWeb like a charm. Select an entry and press the iWeb toolbar button or choose Send to iWeb from the Entry menu. If the entry contains an image Journler includes it with the iWeb post. If the entry contains a movie Journler includes it. Podcast entries are not supported at this time.
